Monday Evenings

Solano Creative Tech Meetup

A community gathering for curious, creative, and civic-minded people in Solano County who want to explore how technology can help us share, create, and solve problems together.

Join the Meetup Learn more →

When

Monday Evenings

Where

Journey Coffee, NavigateRE (downtown), and other spots in Vacaville & Solano County

Who

Everyone welcome. No tech background needed.

What's This About?

This group is for people in Solano County who want to explore how peer-to-peer (P2P) technologies and simple community tools can help us create, share, and solve problems together.

You do not need to be a tech expert. If you're curious, creative, or community-minded, you're already a great fit.

We'll explore ideas like:

Share with each other directly; peer-to-peer.
How neighbors can share information and resources without relying on extractive platforms.
Practical tools for creators.
How artists, makers, and small businesses can use modern tools in practical, low-cost ways.
Stronger local connections.
How technology can support local connections, fairness, and resilience in our communities.
Simple experiments anyone can join.
How to build simple experiments—from shared data to local apps—that anyone can understand and contribute to.

What to Expect

Meetups will be friendly, hands-on, and focused on real local benefits. Whether your background is in tech, art, civic life, small business, education, or you're just curious—come join the conversation.

Friendly

Relaxed atmosphere where all questions are welcome and everyone's perspective matters.

Hands-On

Learn by doing. We'll try things out together and share what works.

Locally Focused

Everything we explore is aimed at real benefits for Solano County communities.

How We Work Together

This is an open, collaborative group. We set the agenda together, shape projects together, and make room for everyone to contribute in ways that fit their time and interests.

Small, Discrete Contributions

Not everyone has hours to spare. We design projects so you can make meaningful contributions in focused bursts—test an idea, share feedback, fix one thing, or help document what we learn.

  • • Try out a tool and share what you notice
  • • Suggest a topic or question to explore
  • • Help write up notes from a session
  • • Connect us with someone who should be in the room

Bigger Picture Thinking

If you want to help shape direction, there's room for that too. Bring your longer-term ideas about what our community needs—we'll figure out together how to move toward them.

  • • Propose a project or experiment
  • • Help prioritize what we work on next
  • • Champion a topic area you care about
  • • Connect our work to broader community goals

Open Agenda, Shared Ownership

We don't want any one person to be a bottleneck. Meeting topics come from the group. Projects are shaped by whoever shows up and contributes. If something matters to you, bring it—we'll make space for it.
